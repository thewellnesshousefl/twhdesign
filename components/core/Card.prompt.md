A warm surface container for grouping content — service tiles, testimonials, form panels. Set `interactive` for clickable cards (adds a calm hover lift).

```jsx
<Card tone="surface" interactive>
  <h3>Massage & Bodywork</h3>
  <p>Slow, intentional bodywork…</p>
</Card>
```

`tone`: `surface` · `sunken` · `sage` · `forest` (dark). `padding`: `none`/`sm`/`md`/`lg`.
