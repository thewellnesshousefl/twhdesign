A styled dropdown for choosing a service, time, or location in booking/intake forms. Matches `Input` exactly.

```jsx
<Select label="Service" placeholder="Choose a service"
  options={['Chiropractic & Fascia Release', 'Massage & Bodywork', 'Wellness Consultation']} />
```

`options` accepts strings or `{value,label}`. Supports `label`, `hint`, `required`, `disabled`.
